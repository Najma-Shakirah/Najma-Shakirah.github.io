Assignment 2: Video

Summary

For this assignment, we were given the task to make a video based on the virtual talk and industrial visit of UTM Digital.

The virtual Talk

The virtual talk was held on 5th December 2023 and moderated by Dr. Aryati. The talk was given by 6 of UTM Digital staffs from different departments namely Mr. Helmee, Mr. Hamri, Ms Aslinda, Mr Mohammed, Mr Khairul Nizam and Mr Noor Haslan. This talk focuses and elaborated on what are their responsibilities working at UTM Digital. This talk contains 5 topics which are UTM Digital Services, Digital Infrastructure, UTM Networking, ICT Security Management, and Project Management and System Development in UTM. The main objective of this talk was to introduce UTM Digital that manage the digital infrastructures of UTM.

The Visit

The industrial visit to UTM Digital were held on 13th December 2023. We also get to see the inner working of UTM Digital. We get to visit the open office, UTM Digital Care, and UTM Data Center.

Video

Huge thanks for my group members (Jia Lin, Ru Qian, Jie Ying, Yi Ya) for working together to create and compile this video!

Reflection

It was an eye-opening experience to be able to see how the UTM Digital. I also got a lot of insights from experts in this field during the visit. I can now visualize of what it would be like to work in an office of a technological organization.
