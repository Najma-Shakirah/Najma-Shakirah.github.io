Assignment 4: Newsletter

Summary

This is a group work where we were tasked to create a newsletter about our visit and what we have learnt during the industrial visit to Petronas and Huawei.

Visiting Petronas

We arrived to KL on 13th November 2023, and arrived at the ExxonMobile building where Petronas is located. We were given light refreshments which were really good! After the meal, were given a talk by Sir Ninderjit Sing and Sir Roman Kvaska, regarding infrastructure services and software engineering. A Q&A session followed, allowing us to ask questions with Petronas experts, seeking clarification on various aspects of the industry.

Visiting Huawei

We get to visit Huawei's technologies showroom were we get to see cutting edge technologies by Huawei. The director came to welcome us and we were presented the devices and computers that were there. My favourite device that was there is the Digital Board because you can draw on it.

Newsletter

In this newsletter, we explained in more detail the summary of the visit, the technologies used from both companies and our reflection on what we got during the visit.

Reflection

As computer science students, it is important that we gain familiarity with both software development and infrastructure management as it is essential for effective and versatile professionals in the technology industry. Thank you Petronas and Huawei team for their warm hospitality and commitment to making this visit a truly enriching experience!
